<p><strong>The Embedded Pico Projectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DLP (Digital Light Processing) Pico Projector</li><li>LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) Pico Projector</li><li>Laser-Beam-Steering (LBS) Pico Projector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded pico projectors market features several types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) pico projectors utilize micro mirrors to create images, offering high brightness and color accuracy, making them suitable for various applications. LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) pico projectors use liquid crystals on a reflective silicon backing, providing superior resolution and contrast, ideal for detailed graphics. Laser-Beam-Steering (LBS) pico projectors employ laser light and MEMS technology for rapid image projection, delivering compact designs with high-definition output in mobile devices.</p></p>
