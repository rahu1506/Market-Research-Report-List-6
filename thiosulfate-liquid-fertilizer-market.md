<p><ul><li>ATS</li><li>PTS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thiosulfate Liquid Fertilizer market comprises two main types: ATS (Ammonium Thiosulfate) and PTS (Potassium Thiosulfate). ATS is primarily used for its nitrogen and sulfur content, enhancing crop yields and soil health. PTS, on the other hand, provides both potassium and sulfur, promoting overall plant growth and improving fruit quality. Both types are beneficial in various agricultural applications, with ATS often preferred for its cost-effectiveness, while PTS is favored for its additional potassium benefits in nutrient-deficient soils.</p></p>
