<p><strong>The Integrated Starter Generator (ISG) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Series Parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Starter Generator (ISG) market comprises three main hybrid configurations. In series hybrids, the ISG acts as both starter and generator, powering the vehicle indirectly through an electric motor. Parallel hybrids use the ISG to assist the internal combustion engine directly, improving efficiency and performance. Series-parallel hybrids combine both methods, allowing for flexible power distribution between the engine and electric motor, maximizing efficiency and range. Each type leverages the ISG to enhance fuel economy and reduce emissions.</p></p>
