<p><p>The diabetic foot ulcer (DFU) therapeutic market focuses on three main ulcer types: neuropathic, ischemic, and neuro-ischemic ulcers. Neuropathic ulcers arise from peripheral neuropathy, leading to loss of sensation and increased injury risk. Ischemic ulcers occur due to restricted blood flow, impairing healing. Neuro-ischemic ulcers combine features of both, involving nerve damage and poor circulation. Treatment strategies include wound care, advanced dressings, antibiotics, and surgical interventions to support healing and reduce the risk of complications, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
