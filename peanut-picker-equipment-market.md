<p><p>The peanut picker equipment market includes various applications based on moisture content during harvesting: dry picking, wet picking, and semi-dry picking. Dry picking is suitable for fully matured peanuts, ensuring cleaner harvesting with minimal soil contamination. Wet picking involves harvesting when peanuts have higher moisture, which provides better pod retention but requires robust equipment to handle the moisture. Semi-dry picking strikes a balance, allowing for harvesting when peanuts are partially mature, optimizing yield and quality while maintaining operational efficiency.</p></p>
