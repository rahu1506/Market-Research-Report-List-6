<p><p>The microwave ablation market comprises two primary types: microwave thermotherapy ablation and cold cycle ablation. Microwave thermotherapy ablation utilizes microwave energy to heat and destroy targeted tissue, commonly employed in cancer treatment and various medical conditions. In contrast, cold cycle ablation involves the application of extreme cold to induce cell death, often used in treating cardiovascular diseases and tumors. Both techniques offer minimally invasive options with distinct mechanisms for lesion ablation and patient recovery.</p></p>
