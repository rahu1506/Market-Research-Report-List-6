<p><strong>The Military Riflescope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Telescopic Sight</li><li>Collimating Optical Sight</li><li>Reflex Sight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military riflescope market includes three main types: telescopic sights, collimating optical sights, and reflex sights. Telescopic sights use magnification and reticles for precise aiming over long distances. Collimating optical sights allow for rapid target acquisition by offering a true sight picture without eye relief constraints, often featuring a red dot. Reflex sights are compact and provide a projected reticle on a lens, enabling quick target acquisition while maintaining peripheral awareness. Each type caters to different tactical needs and preferences.</p></p>
