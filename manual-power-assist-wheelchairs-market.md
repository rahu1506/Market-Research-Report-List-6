<p><p>The manual power-assist wheelchair market features two primary types: powerpack and hand bike. Powerpack-type wheelchairs incorporate an external battery system that attaches to a standard manual wheelchair, providing users with additional propulsion assistance and reducing physical strain. Hand bike-type wheelchairs, on the other hand, modify the manual chair by integrating a pedaling mechanism, allowing users to propel themselves using their arms or legs, thus enhancing mobility and independence while maintaining a degree of traditional manual operation.</p></p>
