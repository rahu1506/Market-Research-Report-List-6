<p><p>Pallet covers are protective materials used to shield goods on pallets during storage or transportation. These covers are commonly made from materials like polyethylene, polypropylene, or vinyl, and are available in various sizes and thicknesses to suit different industrial needs. These factors, combined with the growing need for cost-effective and durable packaging solutions, are shaping the future of the pallet covers market.</p></p>
