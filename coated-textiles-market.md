<p><p>The coated textiles market is categorized into three main types: polymer coated, rubber coated, and others. Polymer coated textiles involve applying synthetic polymers to fabrics, enhancing water resistance, durability, and flexibility, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Rubber coated textiles utilize elastomeric coatings, providing excellent waterproofing and abrasion resistance, commonly used in industrial and protective clothing. The "others" category encompasses specialized coatings like metallic or bio-based options, catering to niche applications in automotive, healthcare, and fashion industries, fostering innovation and variety.</p></p>
