<p><p>The toroidal inductors market is divided into three main types: toroidal coils, common mode inductors, and SMT inductors. Toroidal coils are characterized by their doughnut shape, offering high efficiency and minimal electromagnetic interference. Common mode inductors are used to filter noise in power lines, enhancing signal integrity. SMT inductors, designed for surface mount technology, provide compactness and are ideal for modern electronic circuits. Each type caters to specific applications in industries like electronics, telecommunications, and power supply.</p></p>
