<p><strong>The Automated Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automated pool cleaner market is primarily segmented into crawler drive and wheel drive types. Crawler drive cleaners utilize tracks for enhanced maneuverability, effectively handling uneven surfaces and obstacles in pools. They offer superior climbing capabilities and stability. In contrast, wheel drive cleaners use wheels for movement, making them faster and easier to handle on smooth surfaces. While wheel drives are more suitable for flat-bottom pools, crawler drives excel in more complex pool designs, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
