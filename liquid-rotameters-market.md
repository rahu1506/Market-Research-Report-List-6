<p><p>Liquid rotameters are flow measurement devices used to measure the flow rate of liquids in a variety of industries, including chemicals, water treatment, and pharmaceuticals. They operate on the principle of a float rising or falling in a tapered tube as the flow rate changes, providing a visual indication of flow levels. The simplicity and reliability of liquid rotameters make them popular for both laboratory and industrial applications.</p><p>The Liquid Rotameters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for precise flow measurement in various sectors.
