<p><p>The electric shelling machine market is primarily divided into two categories: vertical type and horizontal type shelling machines. Vertical type machines are designed for efficient processing of various products, offering a smaller footprint and often enhanced gravity-based shelling capabilities. On the other hand, horizontal type machines are known for their higher throughput and suitability for larger-scale operations, typically providing a linear feed system that optimizes shelling efficiency. Both types cater to diverse agricultural needs, enhancing productivity.</p></p>
