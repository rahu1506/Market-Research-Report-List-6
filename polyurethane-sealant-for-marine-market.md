<p><p>Polyurethane sealants for the marine market are categorized into two main types: single component and multi-component. </p><p>Single component sealants are ready to use and cure through moisture in the air, making them convenient and ideal for various marine applications. In contrast, multi-component sealants require mixing two or more components before application, allowing for enhanced customization and properties such as higher strength and durability. Both types are essential for waterproofing, adhesion, and protecting marine vessels against harsh conditions.</p></p>
