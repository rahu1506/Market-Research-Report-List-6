<p><p>3D CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) systems are advanced imaging modalities that provide high-resolution three-dimensional representations of dental and maxillofacial structures. They are widely utilized in dental practices for applications such as implant planning, orthodontics, and surgical guidance.</p><p>The 3D CBCT/Cone Beam CT Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period. Additionally, the growing awareness among healthcare professionals about the benefits of 3D imaging, coupled with its ability to enhance treatment planning and patient outcomes, further contributes to market expansion.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a notable shift towards integration with digital workflows, including CAD/CAM technology.
