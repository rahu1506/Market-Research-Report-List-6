<p><p>The helicopter rotors market encompasses two main types: hingeless rotors and bearingless rotors. In contrast, bearingless rotors utilize composite materials to integrate components, minimizing weight and mechanical complexity while enhancing performance. Both types offer advantages in terms of aerodynamics, noise reduction, and efficiency, catering to the evolving demands of the aerospace industry and improving helicopter flight capabilities.</p></p>
