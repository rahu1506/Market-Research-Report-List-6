<p><p>Filter sand is primarily categorized into two main types: silica sand and zeolite sand. Silica sand, known for its high purity and uniformity, is widely used in water filtration and construction applications due to its effective filtration properties. Zeolite sand, on the other hand, possesses unique ion-exchange and adsorption capabilities, making it ideal for specialized water treatment processes, particularly in removing contaminants. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining water quality across various industrial and municipal applications.</p></p>
