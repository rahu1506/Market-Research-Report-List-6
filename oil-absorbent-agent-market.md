<p><p>The Oil Absorbent Agent Market comprises three primary types: natural organic, natural inorganic, and synthetics. Natural organic absorbents, derived from materials like peat or cellulose, are biodegradable and environmentally friendly. Natural inorganic absorbents, such as clay or diatomaceous earth, harness mineral properties for effective oil absorption. Synthetic absorbents, usually made from polymers or other manufactured materials, offer high absorption capacity and durability. Together, these diverse agents play a crucial role in environmental cleanup and industrial applications, tackling oil spills and contamination.</p></p>
