<p><p>The high precision plastic lens market primarily comprises aspheric and spherical lenses. Aspheric lenses feature a non-spherical surface that minimizes optical aberrations, allowing for improved image quality and thinner profiles, making them ideal for applications in cameras and eyeglasses. In contrast, spherical lenses have uniform curvature and are simpler in design, commonly used in basic optical devices. Both types cater to diverse industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare, driven by the demand for lightweight and cost-effective optical solutions.</p></p>
