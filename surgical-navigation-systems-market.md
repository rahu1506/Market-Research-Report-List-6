<p><p>Surgical navigation systems enhance the precision of surgical procedures by providing real-time guidance. Optical surgical navigation systems utilize cameras and infrared markers to track instruments and anatomical structures, offering high accuracy in open and minimally invasive surgeries. Electromagnetic surgical navigation systems use magnetic fields to determine instrument location, allowing for greater maneuverability in complex procedures. Other market types may include ultrasonic and hybrid systems, integrating various technologies to improve surgical outcomes and enhance the surgeon's ability to visualize and navigate the surgical site.</p></p>
