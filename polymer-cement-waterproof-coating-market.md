<p><p>The Polymer Cement Waterproof Coating market is categorized into two main types based on elongation and poly-ash ratio. Type I coatings feature high elongation and a high poly-ash ratio, offering enhanced flexibility and crack-bridging capabilities, making them ideal for dynamic surfaces. In contrast, Type II coatings exhibit low elongation and a low poly-ash ratio, providing a more rigid structure suited for stable surfaces where less movement is anticipated. Both types serve distinct applications in construction and infrastructure protection.</p></p>
