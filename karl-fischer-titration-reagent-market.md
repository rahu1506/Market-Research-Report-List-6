<p><strong>The Karl Fischer Titration Reagent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Reagent</li><li>Coulometric Reagent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Karl Fischer Titration Reagent Market is primarily categorized into two types: volumetric and coulometric reagents. Volumetric reagents are used for titrations that involve measuring the volume of reagent required to react with water in a sample, typically suited for higher moisture content samples. Coulometric reagents, on the other hand, generate iodine electrochemically, making them ideal for trace moisture analysis in samples with very low water content. Both types are essential for accurate moisture determination in various industries.</p></p>
