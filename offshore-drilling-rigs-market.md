<p><p>The offshore drilling rigs market comprises three main types: Jackup Offshore Drilling Rigs, Semi-Submersibles, and Drillships. Jackup rigs are mobile and suitable for shallow waters, featuring legs that rest on the seabed. Semi-Submersibles float on the water's surface and are anchored, providing stability in deeper waters. Drillships, equipped with advanced technology, are specialized vessels designed for deepwater drilling operations, offering mobility and versatility. Each type serves distinct operational needs based on water depth and environmental conditions.</p></p>
