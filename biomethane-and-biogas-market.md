<p><p>Biomethane and biogas are renewable energy sources produced from organic materials through anaerobic digestion or fermentation. Additionally, the rising awareness of waste management and circular economy principles is promoting the utilization of organic waste for energy production.</p><p>Key trends include increased investments in infrastructure for biogas production, government incentives for renewable energy adoption, and collaborations between public and private sectors.
