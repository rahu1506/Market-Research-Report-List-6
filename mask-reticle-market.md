<p><strong>The Mask Reticle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Mask</li><li>Soda Mask</li><li>Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mask reticle market consists of three primary types: quartz masks, soda-lime glass masks, and film masks. Quartz masks, known for their durability and excellent thermal stability, are commonly used in advanced lithography processes. Soda-lime glass masks are cost-effective and suitable for lower precision applications. Film masks, typically made from polymer materials, offer flexibility and ease of use but are less durable. Each type serves specific needs in semiconductor manufacturing, impacting the precision and efficiency of circuit patterning.</p></p>
