<p><strong>The Photovoltaic DC Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Pole</li><li>4 Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic DC isolators market is divided into two main types: 2-pole and 4-pole isolators. 2-pole isolators are typically used in single-phase solar systems, providing a simple mechanism to disconnect the array from the inverter. In contrast, 4-pole isolators are designed for three-phase systems, allowing for disconnection of multiple circuits simultaneously, enhancing safety and compliance with regulations. Both types play a critical role in ensuring safe operation and maintenance of solar power installations by preventing electrical hazards.</p></p>
