<p><strong>The Automotive TVS Diode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive TVS diode market is primarily segmented into uni-polar and bi-polar types. Uni-polar TVS diodes focus on protecting circuits by clamping positive transients while allowing negative voltages to pass, making them ideal for high-voltage environments. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes provide protection for both positive and negative transients, offering a more comprehensive solution for bidirectional applications. Both types enhance circuit reliability in automotive applications, safeguarding against voltage spikes and ensuring the longevity of electronic components.</p></p>
