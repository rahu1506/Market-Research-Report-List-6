<p><p>Structural Insulating Panels (SIPs) are high-performance building materials that use a core of insulation sandwiched between two structural facings. The market types include Expanded Polystyrene (EPS), Extruded Polystyrene (XPS), and Polyurethane (PUR). EPS is lightweight, cost-effective, and provides good insulation. XPS offers higher compressive strength and moisture resistance, making it suitable for below-grade applications. PUR, known for its superior thermal efficiency, is often used in specialized applications, providing excellent insulation and structural integrity in energy-efficient buildings.</p></p>
