<p><p>Sheet Molding Composite (SMC) is a type of fiber-reinforced polymer that consists of a thermosetting resin blend reinforced with glass fibers. The SMC manufacturing process involves molding sheets of the composite material, which can be shaped into various forms for diverse applications.</p><p>The Sheet Molding Composite (SMC) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.1% during the forecast period.
