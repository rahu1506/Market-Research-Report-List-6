<p><p>The polyurethane vacuum chuck market is primarily categorized into two types: rotary and fixed. Rotary vacuum chucks are designed for dynamic applications, allowing for smooth, continuous rotation of workpieces, making them ideal for machining and blending operations. In contrast, fixed vacuum chucks provide a stable, stationary platform for holding workpieces securely during tasks like milling or sanding. Both types utilize polyurethane materials for durability and effective sealing, ensuring reliable performance across various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
