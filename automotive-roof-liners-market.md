<p><ul><li>Thermoplastics Roof Liners</li><li>Thermoset Roof Liners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive roof liners market comprises two main types: thermoplastics and thermoset roof liners. Thermoplastic roof liners are lightweight, flexible, and can be easily molded, making them suitable for mass production and recycling. They offer good impact resistance and can be custom-colored. On the other hand, thermoset roof liners are known for their durability and heat resistance, providing superior structural integrity. These are often used in high-end vehicles for their aesthetic appeal and longevity, enhancing the overall interior quality.</p></p>
