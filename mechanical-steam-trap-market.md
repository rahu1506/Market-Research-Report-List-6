<p><strong>The Mechanical Steam Trap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float type (Lever Float and Free Float)</li><li>Inverted Bucket type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical steam trap market primarily comprises two types: float and inverted bucket traps. Float type traps include lever float and free float designs; lever float traps use a buoyancy mechanism to maintain a sealing valve, while free float traps allow for unrestricted movement, enhancing reliability and performance. Inverted bucket traps utilize a buoyant bucket that rises and falls with condensate levels, ensuring efficient steam release. Each type serves distinct applications, optimizing steam system efficiency and reducing energy losses.</p></p>
