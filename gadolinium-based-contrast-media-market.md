<p><p>The Gadolinium-based Contrast Media market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Extracellular Agents**: These are used primarily in MRI to enhance imaging of tissues and organs by distributing into the extracellular space. </p><p>2. **Blood Pool Agents**: Designed to remain within the vascular system for longer periods, these agents improve the visualization of blood vessels and cardiac structures.</p><p>3. **Hepatobiliary Agents**: These contrast agents are specialized for imaging liver and biliary systems, allowing for detailed assessment of hepatic lesions and bile ducts.</p></p>
