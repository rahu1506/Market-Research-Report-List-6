<p><strong>The Mechanical Liner Hanger Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotating Type</li><li>Non-Rotating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical liner hanger systems are essential in the oil and gas industry for suspending casing strings and facilitating drilling operations. The market is categorized into rotating and non-rotating types. Rotating type liner hangers allow the casing to rotate during drilling, enhancing wellbore stability and cementing efficiency. In contrast, non-rotating type liner hangers are designed to remain stationary, ideal for specific scenarios where rotation may cause issues. Both types ensure secure anchoring and facilitate effective well completion.</p></p>
