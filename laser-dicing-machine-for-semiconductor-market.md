<p><ul><li>Laser Ablation Dicing</li><li>Stealth Dicing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Dicing Machine for the semiconductor market primarily features two types: Laser Ablation Dicing and Stealth Dicing. Laser Ablation Dicing utilizes intense laser beams to remove unwanted material, creating precise cuts in semiconductor wafers. This method is effective for intricate patterns and reduces mechanical stress. Stealth Dicing, on the other hand, employs a laser to create micro-cracks beneath the wafer surface, enabling subsequent separation with minimal damage to the chips. Both techniques enhance efficiency and yield in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
