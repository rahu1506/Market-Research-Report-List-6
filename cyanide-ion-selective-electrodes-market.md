<p><strong>The Cyanide Ion Selective Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystal Membrane</li><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cyanide ion selective electrodes market is categorized into three main types: crystal membrane, liquid membrane, and PVC membrane electrodes. Crystal membrane electrodes utilize a solid crystalline structure to selectively measure cyanide ions, offering high stability and low drift. Liquid membrane electrodes rely on a liquid phase that selectively interacts with cyanide ions, providing fast response times. PVC membrane electrodes incorporate polyvinyl chloride as the medium, which is cost-effective and widely used, although they may have limited sensitivity compared to the other types.</p></p>
