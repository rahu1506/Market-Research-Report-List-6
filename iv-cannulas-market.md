<p><strong>The IV Cannulas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peripheral IV Cannula</li><li>Central Line IV Cannula</li><li>Midline IV Cannula</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IV cannulas market comprises three primary types: Peripheral IV cannulas, Central Line IV cannulas, and Midline IV cannulas. Peripheral IV cannulas are commonly used for short-term access and are inserted into peripheral veins. Central Line IV cannulas are designed for long-term vascular access, inserted into larger central veins, allowing for the delivery of medications, fluids, and nutrition. Midline IV cannulas fall between these two, featuring a longer catheter that provides extended access while remaining in peripheral veins.</p></p>
