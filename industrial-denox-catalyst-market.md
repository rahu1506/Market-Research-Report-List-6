<p><p>The Industrial DeNOx Catalyst Market features two primary types: Honeycomb and Flat catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts, characterized by their cylindrical geometry, offer high surface area and improved gas flow, enhancing nitrogen oxide (NOx) reduction efficiency. They are commonly used in large-scale applications like power plants and industrial facilities. Flat catalysts, with their planar design, are often utilized in smaller or specific applications where space constraints exist. Both types are essential for meeting environmental regulations and improving air quality by minimizing NOx emissions.</p></p>
