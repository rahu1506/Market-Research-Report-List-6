<p><p>The Microfocus X-ray sources market is primarily divided into two types: open-tube and sealed-tube sources. Open-tube X-ray sources utilize a vacuum environment where electrons strike a target material to produce X-rays, allowing for greater flexibility in operations and adjustable beam characteristics. In contrast, sealed-tube sources are pre-contained in a vacuum chamber, ensuring longer lifespan and reliability with reduced maintenance. Each type caters to specific application needs in industries like materials testing, security, and medical imaging.</p></p>
