<p><p>The proppant market consists primarily of three types: Frac Sand Proppant, Resin-coated Proppant, and Ceramic Proppant. Frac Sand Proppant, made from high-purity silica, is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and availability. Resin-coated Proppant features a layer of resin that enhances conductivity and reduces clay swelling, making it suitable for demanding environments. Ceramic Proppant, known for its high strength and resistance to crush, is ideal for deep wells where high pressures exist, offering superior performance but at a higher cost.</p></p>
