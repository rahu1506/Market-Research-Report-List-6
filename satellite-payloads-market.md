<p><p>The satellite payloads market is categorized into three main types based on orbital altitude: LEO, GEO, and MEO. LEO satellites, positioned up to 2,000 km above Earth, are ideal for Earth observation, communications, and IoT applications due to their low latency. GEO satellites orbit at approximately 35,786 km, providing stable coverage for communications and broadcasting. MEO satellites, situated between LEO and GEO (around 2,000 to 35,786 km), are primarily used for navigation systems, such as GPS, balancing coverage and latency.</p></p>
