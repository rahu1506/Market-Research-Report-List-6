<p><p>Industrial UAVs (drones) are categorized into **airplanes** and **multicopters**, each serving distinct purposes. **Airplanes** are fixed-wing drones designed for long-range, high-speed operations, ideal for large-scale mapping, surveying, and agricultural monitoring. They offer extended flight times and efficiency over vast areas. **Multicopters**, with multiple rotors, excel in precision, stability, and vertical takeoff/landing, making them suitable for inspections, surveillance, and close-range tasks. While airplanes prioritize endurance and coverage, multicopters focus on maneuverability and versatility, catering to diverse industrial needs. Both types are integral to modern industries, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
