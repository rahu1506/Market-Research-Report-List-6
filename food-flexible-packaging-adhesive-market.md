<p><ul><li>Solvent Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-free Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food flexible packaging adhesive market encompasses three main types: solvent adhesives, solvent-free adhesives, and waterborne adhesives. Solvent adhesives contain volatile organic compounds, providing strong bonds but requiring careful handling due to environmental concerns. Solvent-free adhesives eliminate solvents altogether, offering a safer alternative with lower emissions. Waterborne adhesives use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly while maintaining effective bonding properties. Each type caters to different packaging needs, balancing performance, safety, and environmental impact.</p></p>
