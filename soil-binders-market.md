<p><p>The soil binders market comprises two main types: chemical and physical binders. Chemical binders, such as polymers, cement, and lime, enhance soil strength and stability through chemical reactions that improve cohesion. Physical binders, on the other hand, involve materials like fibers or geotextiles that physically reinforce soil structure without altering its chemical composition. Both types are critical in construction, erosion control, and landscaping, providing essential support in various applications to enhance soil performance and durability.</p></p>
