<p><p>Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology (INAAT) is a molecular diagnostic technique used to amplify nucleic acids under constant temperature conditions. This technology enables rapid and efficient amplification without the need for thermal cycling, making it especially beneficial for point-of-care testing and field applications. INAAT encompasses various methods, including loop-mediated isothermal amplification (LAMP), nucleic acid sequence-based amplification (NASBA), and others, offering advantages such as simplicity, cost-effectiveness, and high sensitivity.</p><p>The INAAT market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for rapid diagnostic tests, especially in infectious disease detection and molecular biology applications. The market is expanding due to advancements in technology, rising prevalence of infectious diseases, and a growing emphasis on personalized medicine.
