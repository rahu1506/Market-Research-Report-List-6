<p><p>The Insulin Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API) market comprises two primary types: chemical synthetics and animal extracts. Chemical synthetics involve the laboratory production of insulin through recombinant DNA technology, allowing for precise control over the insulin's properties and quality. In contrast, animal extracts are derived from the pancreases of animals, historically used before the advent of synthetic methods. While synthetic insulin dominates the market due to its safety and efficacy, animal extracts still see limited use, particularly in specific patient populations.</p></p>
