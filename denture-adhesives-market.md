<p><strong>The Denture Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Restoration Bonding</li><li>Indirect Restoration Bonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The denture adhesives market includes two primary types: direct restoration bonding and indirect restoration bonding. Direct restoration bonding involves the application of adhesive directly to the denture and the gums, providing an immediate hold for improved stability and comfort. Indirect restoration bonding, on the other hand, refers to adhesives used in the creation of prosthetic devices that are fabricated outside the mouth, enhancing their retention when placed in the oral cavity. Both types aim to ensure secure fit and optimal functionality for denture wearers.</p></p>
