<p><p>The hair dye products market is categorized into three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes offer a short-term color change, typically lasting only a few washes, ideal for those seeking a quick transformation. Semi-permanent dyes provide longer-lasting results, usually fading after several weeks, and are less damaging to hair than permanent options. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft for long-lasting color, ensuring vibrant results that withstand numerous washes but may require more maintenance and care.</p></p>
