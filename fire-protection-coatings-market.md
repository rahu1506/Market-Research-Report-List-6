<p><ul><li>Cementitious Coatings</li><li>Intumescent Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire protection coatings market features two primary types: cementitious and intumescent coatings. Cementitious coatings are made from a mixture of cement, aggregates, and water, providing a durable barrier that protects structural elements from fire damage. In contrast, intumescent coatings expand when exposed to heat, forming a charred layer that insulates the substrate, delaying structural failure. Both types are essential in enhancing the fire resistance of buildings and infrastructure, ensuring safety and compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
