<p><p>The polyarylsulfone market is segmented into four main types: PPSU (polyphenylsulfone), PSU (polysulfone), PEI (polyetherimide), and PESU (polyethersulfone). PPSU is known for its high heat resistance and mechanical strength, making it suitable for medical and aerospace applications. PSU offers excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability, often used in automotive and industrial sectors. PEI boasts superior thermal and electrical properties, ideal for electrical components. PESU combines features of PSU and PEI, providing a balance of strength and thermal stability for advanced engineering applications.</p></p>
