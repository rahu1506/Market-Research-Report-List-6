<p><p>Ceramic-coated cookware refers to pots, pans, and other kitchen utensils that feature a non-stick ceramic coating applied over a metal base, typically aluminum or stainless steel. Ceramic coatings are known for their heat resistance, durability, and ability to provide a non-stick surface without harmful chemicals.</p><p>The ceramic-coated cookware market has been experiencing significant growth due to increasing consumer awareness about health and environmental concerns.
