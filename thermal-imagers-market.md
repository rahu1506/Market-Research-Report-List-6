<p><p>The thermal imager market primarily features two types of sensor technologies: VOx (vanadium oxide) and a-Si (amorphous silicon). VOx sensors offer higher sensitivity and resolution, making them ideal for demanding applications such as security and surveillance, as well as industrial monitoring. In contrast, a-Si sensors are generally more cost-effective and suitable for less demanding applications, allowing for wider adoption in consumer devices and simple thermal imaging tasks. Both types cater to various market segments, balancing performance and affordability.</p></p>
