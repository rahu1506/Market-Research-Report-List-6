<p><strong>The Electric Multiple Units (EMU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Centralized</li><li>Power Decentralized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Multiple Units (EMUs) are train systems comprised of multiple carriages, each equipped with its own propulsion system. They fall into two main market types: power centralized and power decentralized. In a power centralized market, one or more cars are equipped with larger traction systems, powering the entire unit, enhancing efficiency for longer routes. Conversely, in a power decentralized market, each carriage has its own individual traction system, allowing for greater flexibility, easier maintenance, and efficient operation in various configurations.</p></p>
