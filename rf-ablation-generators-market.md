<p><strong>The RF Ablation Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Fashion</li><li>Multi-polar Fashion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF ablation generators are used in medical procedures to destroy tissue using radiofrequency energy. The monopolar fashion involves a single active electrode delivering energy through the patient to a grounding pad, allowing for controlled tissue ablation. In contrast, multi-polar fashion employs multiple electrodes, enabling more localized treatment and reducing collateral damage to surrounding tissues. This enhanced precision leads to improved outcomes in procedures such as tumor ablation and cardiac interventions, driving demand in the RF ablation generators market.</p></p>
