<p><p>The CT high-pressure injectors market is segmented into single head and dual head injectors. Single head CT high-pressure injectors are designed for administering contrast agents in a single stream, ideal for standard imaging procedures. In contrast, dual head CT high-pressure injectors have two independent channels, allowing for simultaneous injections or the use of different contrast agents. This versatility enhances imaging efficiency and productivity, catering to diverse diagnostic requirements and improving patient throughput in medical facilities.</p></p>
