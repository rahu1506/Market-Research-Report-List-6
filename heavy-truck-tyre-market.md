<p><strong>The Heavy Truck Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy truck tire market is primarily segmented into two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature plies arranged at 90 degrees to the tread, offering better fuel efficiency, traction, and longer lifespan, making them increasingly popular in long-haul trucking. In contrast, bias tires have plies that crisscross at angles, providing greater sidewall strength and improved load-carrying capacity, primarily suited for off-road applications and short-distance transportation. Each type serves specific needs within the heavy trucking industry.</p></p>
