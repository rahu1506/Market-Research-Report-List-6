<p><strong>The Inline Density Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inline density sensors market is segmented into contact type and non-contact type sensors. Contact type sensors measure density by direct interaction with the material, ensuring high accuracy but may be limited by wear and contamination. In contrast, non-contact type sensors use techniques like spectroscopy or electromagnetic methods to determine density without physical contact, offering advantages in cleanliness and maintenance. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals, addressing specific measurement needs and operational environments.</p></p>
