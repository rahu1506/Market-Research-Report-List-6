Beam steering systems adjust the direction of the antenna to focus on the GPS signal, effectively mitigating jamming effects. Civilian systems are designed specifically for non-military use, enhancing GPS reliability in applications like navigation and transportation. Together, these systems improve the resilience of GPS against interference, ensuring accurate positioning and timing.</p></p>
