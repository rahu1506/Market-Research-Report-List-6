<p><ul><li>Remote Payment</li><li>NFC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile wallets can be categorized into two main market types: remote payment and NFC (Near Field Communication) payment. Remote payment allows users to make transactions over the internet, typically using apps or websites, facilitating purchases without the need for physical presence. In contrast, NFC payment enables contactless transactions by simply tapping a mobile device near a compatible terminal, enhancing convenience in physical retail settings. Both types streamline payment processes, offering secure and efficient methods for consumers to manage their finances.</p></p>
