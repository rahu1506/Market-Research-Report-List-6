<p><p>The Organic LED market primarily consists of PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) and AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) technologies. PMOLED displays utilize a simpler structure, suitable for smaller screens and offer lower power consumption, making them ideal for devices like wearables and simple displays. In contrast, AMOLED technology features an active matrix structure, enabling faster refresh rates and superior color quality, which is ideal for larger screens such as smartphones and televisions. Both types cater to diverse applications based on performance and energy needs.</p></p>
