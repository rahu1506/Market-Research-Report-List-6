<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU</li><li>POE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential water treatment equipment is categorized into point-of-use (POU) and point-of-entry (POE) systems. POU systems are installed at water outlets, such as faucets, providing purified water for specific needs like drinking and cooking. In contrast, POE systems treat all water entering a home, ensuring that every tap delivers treated water, effectively addressing broader concerns like hard water and contaminants. Both systems cater to varying consumer preferences and needs regarding water quality and convenience in residential settings.</p></p>
