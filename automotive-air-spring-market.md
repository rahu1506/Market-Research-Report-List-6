<p><ul><li>Capsule Air Spring</li><li>Membrane Air Spring</li><li>Compound Air Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air spring market includes three main types: capsule air springs, membrane air springs, and compound air springs. Capsule air springs consist of a flexible, vented design for improved ride comfort and load support, while membrane air springs utilize a single membrane to adjust air pressure and accommodate varying loads. Compound air springs combine both capsule and membrane designs, offering enhanced performance and durability for demanding applications. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements, ensuring optimal suspension performance.</p></p>
