<p><p>The gas meters market consists of two primary types: wet gas meters and dry gas meters. Wet gas meters measure gas flow using liquid, which helps enhance accuracy, making them ideal for applications where humidity levels are high. In contrast, dry gas meters operate without liquid, relying on mechanical components to measure gas volume, making them suitable for a variety of residential and commercial uses. Both types cater to different operational needs, influencing their demand in various sectors.</p></p>
