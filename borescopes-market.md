<p><p>The borescope market primarily comprises two types: flexible and rigid borescopes. Flexible borescopes feature a malleable insertion tube, allowing them to navigate around curves and reach difficult areas, making them ideal for inspecting complex machinery or anatomy in medical applications. Rigid borescopes have a straight, inflexible design, providing high image quality and precise viewing for straightforward inspections, such as in aviation and manufacturing. Both types serve essential roles in various industries, enhancing inspection capabilities and maintenance efficiency.</p></p>
