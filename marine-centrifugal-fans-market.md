<p><strong>The Marine Centrifugal Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Drive</li><li>Belt Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine centrifugal fans market is primarily categorized into two types: direct drive and belt drive systems. Direct drive fans utilize a motor directly connected to the fan shaft, offering higher efficiency and lower maintenance needs. In contrast, belt drive fans use a belt and pulley system to connect the motor to the fan, allowing for flexibility in speed adjustments and reducing motor strain. Each type caters to specific marine applications based on efficiency, space constraints, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
