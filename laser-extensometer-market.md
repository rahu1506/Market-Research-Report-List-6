<p><p>The laser extensometer market consists of two primary types: built-in laser extensometers and frame laser extensometers. Built-in laser extensometers are integrated directly into testing equipment, providing precise measurements in real time without needing external setups, making them ideal for space-constrained environments. In contrast, frame laser extensometers are standalone devices mounted on frames, offering versatility for various testing applications. Both types enhance accuracy in measuring deformation and strain in materials during mechanical testing, catering to different industrial needs.</p></p>
