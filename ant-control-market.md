<p><strong>The Ant Control Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray</li><li>Powder</li><li>Bait</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ant control market comprises three primary types: sprays, powders, and baits. Sprays provide immediate knockdown, targeting visible infestations and offering quick solutions for indoor and outdoor areas. Powders, on the other hand, are effective for long-term control, penetrating cracks and crevices to eliminate hidden ant nests. Baits attract ants to a toxic substance, allowing for the gradual elimination of colonies as worker ants carry the poison back to the nest, ensuring comprehensive eradication. Each type serves distinct needs in pest management.</p></p>
