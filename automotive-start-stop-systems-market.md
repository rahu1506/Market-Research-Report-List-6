<p><p>The automotive start-stop systems market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. In contrast, Type II systems employ advanced technologies, including integrated starter generators (ISGs) and high-capacity batteries, enabling smoother operations and higher fuel efficiency, making them suitable for advanced vehicles and hybrids targeting eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
