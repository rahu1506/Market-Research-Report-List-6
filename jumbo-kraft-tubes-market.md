<p><p>Jumbo Kraft Tubes are categorized into three main types based on their construction: Single Ply, Double Ply, and Triple Ply. Single Ply tubes consist of one layer of kraft paper, offering lightweight and economical solutions for less demanding applications. Double Ply tubes provide added strength and durability with two layers, making them suitable for moderate use. Triple Ply tubes, featuring three layers, offer superior sturdiness and resistance, ideal for heavy-duty applications such as shipping and storage.</p></p>
