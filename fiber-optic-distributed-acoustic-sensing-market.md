<p><p>Fiber Optic Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) market is primarily categorized into two types: Distributed Acoustic Sensing Intrusion (DASI) and Distributed Acoustic Sensing Pipeline (DASP). DASI focuses on security and surveillance applications, utilizing fiber optics to detect intrusions along extended perimeters through acoustic signals. Conversely, DASP is tailored for monitoring pipelines, enabling real-time assessment of conditions and potential leaks by analyzing vibrations and sounds within the pipeline structure. Both types enhance safety and operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
