<p><strong>The Vacuum Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Vacuum Capacitor</li><li>Variable Vacuum Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum capacitor market is primarily divided into two types: fixed vacuum capacitors and variable vacuum capacitors. Fixed vacuum capacitors have a predetermined capacitance value and are used in applications requiring stable voltage levels, such as RF amplifiers and transmitters. In contrast, variable vacuum capacitors allow users to adjust capacitance within a specified range, making them ideal for tuning circuits in radio frequency applications, such as transmitters and receivers. Both types are essential in high-frequency and high-voltage environments.</p></p>
