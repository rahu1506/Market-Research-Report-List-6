<p><p>The marine castings and forgings market comprises two primary segments: marine castings and marine forgings. Marine castings involve the process of pouring molten metal into molds to create complex shapes used in shipbuilding, propellers, and engine components. Marine forgings involve shaping metal using compressive forces, producing strong, durable parts, such as shafts and fittings. Both segments are crucial for manufacturing robust, reliable components that can withstand harsh marine environments, ensuring safety and performance in maritime applications.</p></p>
