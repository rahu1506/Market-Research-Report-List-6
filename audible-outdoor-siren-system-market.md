<p><p>The Audible Outdoor Siren System market is categorized into three main types: Omni-Directional, Rotating Directional, and Fixed Directional systems. Omni-Directional sirens emit sound in all directions, ensuring widespread alert coverage, making them ideal for open areas. Rotating Directional sirens, on the other hand, can pivot to focus sound in specific directions, enhancing alert effectiveness in targeted zones. Fixed Directional systems provide a constant alert in one direction, suitable for specific risk areas, catering to various safety and emergency response needs.</p></p>
