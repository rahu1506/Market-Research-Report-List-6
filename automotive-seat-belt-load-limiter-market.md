<p><p>The automotive seat belt load limiter market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Digressive Load Limiters** - These limit the force applied to the occupant by progressively reducing the load as more force is applied during a crash.</p><p>2. **Progressive Load Limiters** - They gradually increase the load tolerance, allowing a controlled deceleration during a collision, enhancing safety.</p><p>3. **Adaptive/Switchable Load Limiters** - These can adjust their performance based on specific collision conditions or occupant characteristics, providing optimal protection in varying scenarios.</p></p>
