<p><strong>The Non-Woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The non-woven fabric market comprises several key types, each defined by its unique manufacturing process. Meltblown fabrics are created by extruding melted polymers, resulting in fine, high-performance materials often used in filtration. Spunbonded fabrics are produced by spinning fibers onto a substrate, offering durability for various applications. Spunlace involves entangling fibers using water jets, ideal for wipes and medical textiles. Needle punch fabrics are formed by mechanically entangling fibers, commonly used in carpets and insulation. Other types include composite and chemical bonded fabrics, catering to specialized needs.</p></p>
