<p><p>Carbon fiber fabric cloth is categorized based on the number of filaments in each tow, denoted by "k" (thousand). **3k** fabrics have 3,000 filaments per tow, offering a balanced mix of strength and flexibility, ideal for lightweight applications. **6k** fabrics, with 6,000 filaments, provide higher strength and are commonly used in aerospace and automotive industries. **12k** fabrics, with 12,000 filaments, are thicker and used for heavy-duty applications. **Others** include specialized variants like 1k or 24k, tailored for specific performance needs.</p></p>
