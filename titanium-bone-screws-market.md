<p><p>The titanium bone screws market comprises various types, primarily categorized into self-tapping, self-cutting, and others. Self-tapping titanium bone screws are designed to create their own thread in bone, enhancing fixation without the need for pre-drilled holes. Self-cutting titanium bone screws, on the other hand, include a cutting mechanism that allows them to slice through bone tissue as they are inserted, improving stability. Other types may include specialized screws tailored for specific applications or conditions, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>
