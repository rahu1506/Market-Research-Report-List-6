<p><strong>The Moulding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moulding Vertical Moulding Equipment</li><li>Flask Moulding Equipment</li><li>Match Moulding Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The moulding equipment market is classified into three main types: vertical moulding equipment, flask moulding equipment, and match moulding equipment. Vertical moulding equipment offers efficient production for small to medium-sized castings, leveraging a vertical design for space-saving. Flask moulding equipment uses frames (flasks) to hold sand and provide a structured moulding process, ideal for larger, complex castings. Match moulding equipment combines two halves to create uniform moulds quickly, enhancing productivity and accuracy for high-volume casting operations.</p></p>
