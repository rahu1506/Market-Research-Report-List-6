<p><ul><li>Belt Drive</li><li>Direct Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass fans market is divided into two primary types: belt drive and direct drive fans. Belt drive fans utilize a motor that powers a pulley system, allowing for more flexible installation and maintenance, while providing quieter operation and reduced vibrations. In contrast, direct drive fans feature a motor directly connected to the fan blade, resulting in higher efficiency and reduced energy consumption. Both types are popular in various applications due to their durability and effective airflow management.</p></p>
