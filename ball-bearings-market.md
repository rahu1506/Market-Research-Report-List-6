<p><ul><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearing</li><li>Self-aligning Ball Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ball bearings market comprises various types, notably Deep Groove Ball Bearings and Self-aligning Ball Bearings. Deep Groove Ball Bearings are designed for high radial and moderate axial loads, providing versatility in applications from motors to machinery. Self-aligning Ball Bearings, on the other hand, feature a spherical outer race that accommodates misalignment, making them ideal for conditions with shaft deflection or installation errors. Both types are essential in enhancing operational efficiency and reducing friction in mechanical systems.</p></p>
