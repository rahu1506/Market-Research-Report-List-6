<p><p>The CMP slurry market is segmented into three main types: alumina slurry, colloidal silica slurry, and ceria slurries. Alumina slurry is commonly used for polishing silicon wafers due to its effective abrasion properties. Colloidal silica slurry offers fine polishing capabilities, often employed in semiconductor and optical applications for achieving high surface finishes. Ceria slurries are favored for their ability to provide superior removal rates and are typically used in advanced materials processing, such as in glass and ceramics polishing.</p></p>
