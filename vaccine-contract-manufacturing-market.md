<p><p>The vaccine contract manufacturing market is divided into two primary types: bulk product and fill-finish. The bulk product market involves large-scale production of vaccine components before they are formulated, focusing on producing active ingredients in substantial quantities. In contrast, fill-finish encompasses the final stages of vaccine production, where the bulk product is formulated, filled into vials or syringes, and sealed for distribution. Both segments are essential for ensuring the efficient and safe delivery of vaccines to meet global health needs.</p></p>
