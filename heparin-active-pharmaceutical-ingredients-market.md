<p><p>The Heparin Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients market encompasses two main categories: Unfractionated Heparin (UFH) and Low Molecular Weight Heparin (LMWH). UFH is primarily used for immediate anticoagulation in various medical conditions, such as deep vein thrombosis and pulmonary embolism. In contrast, LMWH offers more predictable pharmacokinetics, enabling outpatient treatment and simpler dosing regimens. Both forms are critical in managing anticoagulation therapy, ensuring patient safety, and reducing complications related to thromboembolic events in clinical settings.</p></p>
