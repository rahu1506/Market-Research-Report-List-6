<p><p>The automotive air filters market includes various types designed to enhance air quality within vehicles. Particle filters efficiently capture dust, pollen, and other particulate matter, ensuring cleaner air for passengers. Activated carbon filters specialize in eliminating odors and harmful gases by adsorbing volatile organic compounds. Electrostatic filters utilize static electricity to attract and trap contaminants, providing an added layer of filtration. Together, these filters play a critical role in improving air quality, enhancing comfort, and supporting overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
