<p><p>Medium Voltage Ring Main Units (RMUs) are essential in electrical distribution systems and come in three primary types: Solid Insulated, Gas Insulated, and Air Insulated. Solid Insulated RMUs use solid materials for insulation, offering compact designs and enhanced safety. Gas Insulated RMUs utilize sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation, allowing for smaller installations and high reliability in harsh environments. Air Insulated RMUs rely on air for insulation, are more economical, and are commonly used in urban areas due to their simplicity and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
