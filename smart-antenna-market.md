<p><p>The smart antenna market features two primary types: switched multi-beam antennas and adaptive array antennas. Switched multi-beam antennas focus on directing multiple beams towards different users or areas, enhancing coverage and capacity in wireless networks. In contrast, adaptive array antennas utilize advanced signal processing to dynamically adjust their radiation patterns based on user demand and interference, optimizing performance in real time. Both types significantly improve communication efficiency and reliability, catering to the growing needs of modern wireless systems.</p></p>
