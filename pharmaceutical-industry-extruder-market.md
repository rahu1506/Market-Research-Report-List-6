<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Industry Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry extruder market is categorized into two main types: single screw and twin screw extruders. Single screw extruders feature a single rotating screw that pushes material through a heated barrel, suitable for simpler formulations and processes. In contrast, twin screw extruders consist of two intermeshing screws, allowing for better mixing, flexibility, and control over the process, which is ideal for complex formulations and high shear applications. Both types are essential for drug formulation and processing in pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
