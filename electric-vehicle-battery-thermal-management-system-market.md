<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Battery Thermal Management System market is categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I systems primarily utilize passive cooling methods, relying on materials with high thermal conductivity to regulate battery temperature without active components. In contrast, Type II systems incorporate active cooling methods, such as liquid cooling or forced air, providing efficient temperature control for high-performance batteries. Both types are essential for enhancing battery life, performance, and safety in electric vehicles, catering to diverse operational requirements.</p></p>
