<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Micro Dispensing Systems</li><li>Contact Micro Dispensing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro dispensing systems are categorized into two main types: non-contact and contact systems. Non-contact micro dispensing systems utilize methods like jetting, allowing precise dispensing without touching the substrate, making them ideal for delicate components. In contrast, contact micro dispensing systems involve physical contact with the surface for material application, often using needles or tips. Each type serves different applications based on requirements like viscosity, material properties, and substrate sensitivity, catering to industries such as electronics, pharmaceuticals, and automotive.</p></p>
