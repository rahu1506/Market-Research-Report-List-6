<p><strong>The Frac Plugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Composite Plugs</li><li>Dissolvable Plugs</li><li>Cast Iron Plugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frac plugs market is categorized into three main types: composite plugs, dissolvable plugs, and cast iron plugs. Composite plugs are lightweight and designed for easy deployment, providing effective pressure sealing in hydraulic fracturing. Dissolvable plugs are made from materials that dissolve in fluids, allowing for seamless removal after use, minimizing environmental impact. Cast iron plugs, known for their durability and strength, serve in high-pressure applications, providing reliable isolation. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to specific needs in fracturing operations.</p></p>
