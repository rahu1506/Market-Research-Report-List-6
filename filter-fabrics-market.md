<p><p>The filter fabrics market can be categorized into several types, including woven and non-woven filter fabrics. Woven filter fabrics are made by interlacing yarns, providing strength and durability, while non-woven fabrics are created through bonding processes, offering flexibility and efficiency. Additionally, polypropylene (PP), polyester, and nylon filter fabrics are prominent, each exhibiting unique properties. PP filter fabric is known for its chemical resistance, polyester offers high tensile strength, and nylon provides excellent abrasion resistance, catering to diverse filtration applications.</p></p>
