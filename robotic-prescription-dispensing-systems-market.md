<p><p>Robotic Prescription Dispensing Systems streamline the medication distribution process in healthcare settings. Centralized dispensing systems operate from a central location, managing large volumes of prescriptions efficiently, which enhances inventory control and reduces errors. Conversely, decentralized dispensing systems are implemented closer to the point of care, enabling immediate access to medications at different departments or units within a facility. Both types aim to optimize workflow, improve patient safety, and ensure accurate medication delivery, catering to various operational needs of healthcare providers.</p></p>
