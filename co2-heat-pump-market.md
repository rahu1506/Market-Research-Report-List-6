<p><strong>The CO2 Heat Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air to Water Monobloc</li><li>Air to Water Cylinder</li><li>Air to Water Split</li><li>Exhaust Air</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 heat pump market includes several types of systems tailored for different applications. **Air to Water Monobloc** units integrate all components in one outdoor unit, providing efficiency and ease of installation. **Air to Water Cylinder** systems combine a heat pump with a hot water tank, ideal for residential use. **Air to Water Split** configurations feature separate indoor and outdoor units, allowing flexible installations. **Exhaust Air** systems utilize waste heat from ventilation to enhance energy efficiency, ideal for retrofitting existing buildings.</p></p>
