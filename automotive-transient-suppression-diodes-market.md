<p><p>The automotive transient suppression diodes market is primarily categorized into uni-polar and bi-polar types. Uni-polar diodes allow current to flow in one direction, making them effective for voltage clamping and protecting circuits from positive transients. In contrast, bi-polar diodes can conduct in both directions, offering greater versatility for handling transient events in various automotive applications. Both types play crucial roles in safeguarding electronic components against voltage spikes, thus ensuring the reliability and longevity of automotive systems.</p></p>
