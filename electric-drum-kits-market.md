<p><p>The electric drum kits market comprises three main types based on drumhead materials: rubber heads, Mylar heads, and mesh or silicone heads. Rubber heads offer durability and affordability, making them suitable for beginners. Mylar heads, traditionally used in acoustic drums, provide a more realistic feel and sound but may be less common in electric kits. Mesh or silicone heads are favored for their responsive playability, silence, and realistic bounce, appealing to serious drummers seeking a premium experience. Each type caters to different preferences and playing styles.</p></p>
