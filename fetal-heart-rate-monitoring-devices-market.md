<p><p>Fetal heart rate monitoring devices are essential for assessing fetal well-being during pregnancy and labor. The Doppler ultrasound device utilizes high-frequency sound waves to detect and amplify the fetal heartbeat, providing a non-invasive and portable method for monitoring. On the other hand, electronic fetal monitoring devices continuously track fetal heart rate and uterine contractions through external or internal sensors, offering real-time data for clinicians during labor. Both types help in identifying potential complications and ensuring timely medical intervention.</p></p>
