<p><p>The membrane pleated filter market includes various types such as polypropylene (PP) filters, polyethersulfone (PES) filters, polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) filters, and nylon filters. PP filters are known for their chemical resistance and affordability, making them ideal for general liquid filtration. PES filters offer high flow rates and thermal stability, suitable for challenging applications. PTFE filters are hydrophobic, suitable for gas and aggressive liquid filtration. Nylon filters provide strong mechanical properties and versatility in various filtration processes, catering to both industrial and laboratory needs.</p></p>
