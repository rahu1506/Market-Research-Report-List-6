<p><p>The automotive ignition system comprises key components essential for engine operation. The **ignition coil** transforms low-voltage power into high-voltage sparks. The **distributor** routes high-voltage current to the correct spark plug at the right time. The **rotor**, inside the distributor, spins to transfer voltage to spark plug terminals. **Spark plugs** ignite the air-fuel mixture in the combustion chamber. **Other** components include ignition modules, wires, and sensors, ensuring efficient combustion and engine performance. These parts work together to optimize vehicle ignition and overall functionality.</p></p>
