<p><strong>The Swimming Pool Treadmills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Treadmills</li><li>Electrodynamic Treadmills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swimming pool treadmills market includes two primary types: mechanical treadmills and electrodynamic treadmills. Mechanical treadmills operate using a belt system that is powered by the user's movement, providing a low-tech and often more affordable option. In contrast, electrodynamic treadmills utilize electric motors to adjust the speed and resistance dynamically, offering a more versatile and technologically advanced workout experience. Both types cater to fitness enthusiasts seeking a unique exercise regimen in water, promoting low-impact workouts and rehabilitation.</p></p>
