<p><p>The Exhaust Heat Recovery System (EHRS) market encompasses several technologies aimed at harnessing waste heat from exhaust gases to improve overall energy efficiency. Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery systems capture heat for preheating or power generation. Rankine Cycle Systems convert waste heat into mechanical energy using a working fluid. Thermoelectric generators produce electricity from temperature gradients created by exhaust heat. Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) employs an electric motor to convert exhaust energy into additional power, enhancing engine performance and efficiency.</p></p>
