<p><p>The indoor optical cable market is categorized into three main types: single core, double core, and multi-core cables. Single core cables consist of one optical fiber, ideal for simple, short-distance connections. Double core cables feature two fibers, offering redundancy and increased data transmission capacity for slightly more complex setups. Multi-core cables contain multiple fibers, allowing for high data bandwidth and extensive networking capabilities, making them suitable for large-scale applications in commercial buildings and data centers.</p></p>
