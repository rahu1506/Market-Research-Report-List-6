<p><ul><li>Interferometric Fiber Optic Gyroscope</li><li>Resonant Fiber OpticGyro</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser gyroscope market is primarily categorized into two types: Interferometric Fiber Optic Gyroscopes (IFOG) and Resonant Fiber Optic Gyroscopes (RFOG). IFOGs utilize interference patterns of light to detect angular motion, making them highly accurate and stable, suitable for applications in aerospace and navigation. RFOGs, on the other hand, rely on the resonance of light within the fiber to measure rotation rates, offering advantages in size and power efficiency, commonly used in guidance systems and robotics.</p></p>
