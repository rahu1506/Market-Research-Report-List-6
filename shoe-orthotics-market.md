<p><p>The shoe orthotics market is primarily categorized into rigid and soft orthotics. Rigid orthotics are made from firm materials, providing strong support and correction for conditions like flat feet or high arches. They focus on realigning foot structure and improving stability. In contrast, soft orthotics are crafted from cushioned materials, offering comfort and shock absorption. They are ideal for managing conditions like plantar fasciitis or arthritis, providing relief while promoting proper foot mechanics. Both types cater to diverse needs and preferences.</p></p>
