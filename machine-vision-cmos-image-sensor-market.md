<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Machine Vision CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Shutter</li><li>Global Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Machine Vision CMOS Image Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Rolling Shutter and Global Shutter. Rolling Shutter captures images sequentially line by line, making it suitable for static scenes but prone to distortions with fast-moving objects. In contrast, Global Shutter captures the entire image simultaneously, eliminating motion artifacts, making it ideal for high-speed applications. Each type serves distinct applications based on the requirements of speed, distortion tolerance, and overall imaging quality in machine vision systems.</p></p>
