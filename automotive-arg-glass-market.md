<p><p>The Automotive ARG (Automotive Replacement Glass) market comprises three primary types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass, known for its strength and safety, is commonly used in side windows due to its resistance to shattering. Laminated glass features a layer of plastic between two glass panes, providing enhanced security and sound insulation, often used in windshields. The "others" category includes specialized glass types, such as tinted or heated glass, which cater to specific functional and aesthetic needs in vehicles.</p></p>
