<p><p>The flue exhaust muffler market is divided into hot and cold flue exhaust mufflers, catering to different applications. Hot flue exhaust mufflers are designed to handle high-temperature exhaust gases, commonly used in industrial settings, boilers, and marine engines. They minimize noise and vibration from hot exhaust systems. In contrast, cold flue exhaust mufflers manage lower-temperature exhaust, typically found in residential applications, ensuring efficient sound reduction while maintaining optimal exhaust flow. Both types enhance performance and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
