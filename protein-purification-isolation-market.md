<p><p>Protein purification involves techniques to isolate proteins from complex mixtures for research or therapeutic purposes. Key market types include ultrafiltration, which uses membrane processes to separate proteins based on size; precipitation, where solvents induce protein aggregation; centrifugation, which separates components based on density; chromatography, that separates proteins based on chemical properties; and electrophoresis, which utilizes electric fields for separation by size or charge. Additional methods may include affinity purification and dialysis, expanding the options available in the protein purification market.</p></p>
