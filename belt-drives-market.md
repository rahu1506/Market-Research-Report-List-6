<p><p>Belt drives are classified into several market types based on their design and application: </p><p>1. **ZV (Variable Speed)**: These systems allow adjustable speed for flexible operation. </p><p>2. **CV (Constant Velocity)**: Maintains consistent speed, ideal for precise applications. </p><p>3. **CR (Constant Ratio)**: Ensures a fixed ratio between input and output speeds, suitable for specific tasks. </p><p>4. **CL (Constant Load)**: Designed to maintain torque and power under varying loads, crucial for heavy-duty uses. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs and efficiencies.</p></p>
