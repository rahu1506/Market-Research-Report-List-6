<p><p>The veterinary dental elevator market encompasses various types of elevators used for tooth extractions and oral surgical procedures in animals. Standard elevators are characterized by their simple design, featuring a straight blade that aids in loosening teeth within the socket. Winged elevators, on the other hand, have a broader, wing-like blade that provides enhanced leverage and stability, making them suitable for challenging extractions. Both types play a crucial role in veterinary dentistry, improving efficiency and outcomes during dental surgeries.</p></p>
