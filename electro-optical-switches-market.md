<p><ul><li>Single-mode Switches</li><li>Multimode Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electro-Optical Switches market comprises two primary types: single-mode switches and multimode switches. Single-mode switches are designed for high-performance applications, utilizing a single light pathway for data transmission, enabling long-distance communication with minimal signal loss. In contrast, multimode switches accommodate multiple light pathways, making them suitable for shorter distances and offering higher bandwidth over localized networks. Both switch types play crucial roles in enhancing connectivity, supporting diverse applications in telecommunications, data centers, and optical networks.</p></p>
