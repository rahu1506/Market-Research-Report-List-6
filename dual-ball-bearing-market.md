<p><p>The dual ball bearing market can be categorized into two main types based on material: metal and non-metallic materials. Metal dual ball bearings, typically made from steel or stainless steel, offer high durability, strength, and resistance to wear and high temperatures, making them suitable for demanding applications. Non-metallic dual ball bearings, made from plastics or composites, provide advantages such as corrosion resistance, lighter weight, and lower friction coefficients, making them ideal for applications requiring less weight and enhanced chemical resistance.</p></p>
