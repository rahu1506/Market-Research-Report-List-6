<p><p>Neurostimulation devices encompass various therapeutic technologies targeting different neurological and physiological conditions. Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) treats movement disorders like Parkinson's by delivering electrical impulses to specific brain regions. Sacral Nerve Stimulation (SNS) addresses urinary incontinence and bowel dysfunction through nerve stimulation. Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS) manages epilepsy and depression by influencing brain activity. Other market devices encompass emerging technologies and treatments.</p></p>
