<p><p>Valves are essential components in various industries, classified into several market types based on their function. Gate valves are used for on/off service, allowing full flow with minimal pressure loss. Control valves regulate flow and pressure, crucial for process control. Ball valves provide quick shut-off with low resistance when fully open. Butterfly valves offer a compact design for isolating or regulating flow in large pipes. Other valves include check valves and strainers, each serving specialized functions in fluid management systems.</p></p>
