<p><ul><li>Polyether Polyols</li><li>Polyester Polyols</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyether polyols and polyester polyols are key raw materials in the polyurethane industry. Polyether polyols, derived from alkylene oxides, offer flexibility, hydrolysis resistance, and low viscosity, making them ideal for flexible foams, coatings, and adhesives. Polyester polyols, synthesized from dicarboxylic acids and glycols, provide superior mechanical strength, thermal stability, and chemical resistance, often used in rigid foams, elastomers, and coatings. Both types cater to diverse applications, with polyether polyols dominating flexible products and polyester polyols excelling in durable, high-performance materials.</p></p>
