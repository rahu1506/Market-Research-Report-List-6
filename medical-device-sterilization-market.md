<p><strong>The Medical Device Sterilization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Sterilization</li><li>Ethylene Oxide (EO)</li><li>Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor and Plasma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical device sterilization market encompasses several key methods. Steam sterilization utilizes high-pressure steam to eliminate microorganisms, commonly used for heat-resistant instruments. Ethylene Oxide (EO) sterilization employs a gas that penetrates materials at low temperatures, ideal for sensitive devices. Hydrogen peroxide vapor sterilization combines vaporized hydrogen peroxide with low heat to disinfect complex devices. Lastly, plasma sterilization uses low-temperature gas plasmas to effectively sterilize heat-labile items, offering rapid cycles and minimal residues, making it suitable for delicate medical tools.</p></p>
