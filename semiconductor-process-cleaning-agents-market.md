<p><p>The semiconductor process cleaning agents market comprises various types, primarily categorized into acid cleaning agents, alkaline cleaning agents, and others. Acid cleaning agents, typically containing sulfuric or nitric acid, effectively remove metal ions and organic contaminants from substrates. Alkaline cleaning agents, often based on caustic soda or ammonium hydroxide, are utilized to eliminate organic residues. The "others" category includes specialized cleaners, such as environmentally friendly and enzymatic solutions, catering to specific cleaning needs in semiconductor manufacturing. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring process integrity and product quality.</p></p>
