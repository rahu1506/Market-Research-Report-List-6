<p><p>High Density PTFE Tapes are specialized tapes used in various industrial applications. The market is divided into two main types: Ordinary and High-Temperature Resistance tapes. Ordinary PTFE tapes are commonly used for standard sealing applications due to their chemical resistance and low friction properties. In contrast, High-Temperature Resistance PTFE tapes are designed to withstand extreme temperatures, making them ideal for demanding environments such as automotive and aerospace sectors. Both types enhance performance and reliability in sealing and insulation tasks.</p></p>
