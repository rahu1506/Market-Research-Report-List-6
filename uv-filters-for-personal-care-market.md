<p><ul><li>Mineral UV Filters</li><li>Organic UV Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The personal care market features two primary types of UV filters: mineral and organic. Mineral UV filters, such as zinc oxide and titanium dioxide, physically block and reflect UV rays, making them suitable for sensitive skin and offering broad-spectrum protection. Organic UV filters, on the other hand, are chemical compounds that absorb UV radiation, transforming it into heat, which is then released from the skin. Both types are used in sunscreens and skincare formulations to enhance skin protection against sun damage.</p></p>
