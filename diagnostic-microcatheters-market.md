<p><p>Diagnostic microcatheters are specialized medical devices used for precise navigation and imaging in minimally invasive procedures. Single lumen microcatheters feature one channel, enabling targeted delivery of contrast agents or medications, ideal for straightforward diagnostic tasks. Double lumen microcatheters have two channels, allowing simultaneous functions like fluid delivery and pressure monitoring, suited for complex interventions. Both types enhance procedural accuracy, with single lumen focusing on simplicity and double lumen offering versatility for advanced diagnostic and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
