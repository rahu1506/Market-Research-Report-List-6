<p><p>The binders for batteries market is segmented into anode and cathode binders, each serving distinct functions in battery performance. Anode binders enhance the adhesion of active materials in the anode, improving the overall conductivity and stability of lithium-ion batteries. Cathode binders, on the other hand, facilitate the binding of active materials in the cathode, crucial for energy density and cycle life. Both types play vital roles in ensuring battery efficiency, longevity, and overall energy storage performance.</p></p>
