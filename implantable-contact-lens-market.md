<p><strong>The Implantable Contact Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Myopia Contact Lens</li><li>Hyperopia Contact Lenses</li><li>Astigmatism Contact Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable contact lens market includes three primary types: myopia contact lenses for correcting nearsightedness, hyperopia contact lenses for addressing farsightedness, and astigmatism contact lenses designed to correct irregularities in the eye's curvature. Myopia lenses help improve distant vision, while hyperopia lenses enhance near vision. Astigmatism lenses provide clarity for both near and far distances by compensating for unequal curvature. These lenses offer a permanent solution to refractive errors, appealing to patients seeking alternatives to traditional glasses and contact lenses.</p></p>
