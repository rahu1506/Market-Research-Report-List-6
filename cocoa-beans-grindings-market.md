<p><p>The cocoa bean grinding market consists of three main products: cocoa liquor, cocoa butter, and cocoa powder. Cocoa liquor, a paste made from ground cocoa beans, serves as the foundation for chocolate production. Cocoa butter, extracted from the beans, is a fat used in chocolate and cosmetics for its smooth texture and moisturizing properties. Cocoa powder, produced by removing most of the fat from cocoa liquor, is utilized in baking and beverages, adding flavor and color to various products.</p></p>
