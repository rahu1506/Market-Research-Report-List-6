<p><p>Electric Vehicle Battery Management Systems (BMS) can be categorized into three main market types: distributed, centralized, and modular. Distributed BMS features individual monitoring for each battery cell, enhancing reliability and performance management. Centralized BMS consolidates control functions in a single unit, simplifying the design and reducing costs. Modular BMS combines elements of both, allowing for scalability and flexibility in various vehicle designs. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to different vehicle requirements and production scales.</p></p>
