<p><strong>The Automotive Plastic Injection Molding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The **Automotive Plastic Injection Molding Market** includes various molding techniques. **Thermoplastic Injection Molding** uses heat to melt plastic, which is then injected into molds to form parts. **Cold Runner Molding** involves a system where the plastic cools in the runner, reducing waste but requiring manual removal. **Hot Runner Molding** keeps the plastic molten in the runner, enabling faster cycles and less waste. **Others** encompass specialized methods like gas-assisted or multi-material molding, catering to unique automotive part requirements.</p></p>
