<p><ul><li>Continuous Water Cycle</li><li>No Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water Belt Cooler market is divided into two primary types: Continuous Water Cycle and No Loop systems. Continuous Water Cycle coolers utilize a steady flow of water, effectively dissipating heat and maintaining optimal temperatures through constant circulation. In contrast, No Loop systems operate without recirculating water, relying on a static source for cooling. These systems often require less maintenance and are easier to install, making them suitable for specific applications where consistent cooling is essential.</p></p>
