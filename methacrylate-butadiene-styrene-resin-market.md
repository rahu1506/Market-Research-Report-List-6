<p><p>The Methacrylate Butadiene Styrene (MBS) resin market is divided into two main types: transparent and non-transparent. Transparent MBS resins are valued for their clarity and are commonly used in applications that require visibility, such as optical lenses, display housings, and packaging. Non-transparent MBS resins, on the other hand, typically prioritize strength and impact resistance, making them suitable for automotive parts, electrical components, and structural applications where aesthetics are less important. Both types cater to distinct industry needs.</p></p>
