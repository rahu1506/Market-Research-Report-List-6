<p><p>The nephro genetic testing market comprises various methodologies to identify genetic factors associated with kidney diseases. Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) allows for comprehensive, high-throughput analysis of genetic variations, enabling the detection of multiple mutations simultaneously. In contrast, Multiplex Ligation-dependent Probe Amplification (MLPA) is a targeted technique that amplifies specific DNA sequences, making it efficient for detecting deletions or duplications in genes related to kidney disorders. Together, these approaches enhance the understanding and diagnosis of renal genetic conditions.</p></p>
