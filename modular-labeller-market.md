<p><p>The modular labeller market encompasses various labeling technologies, with straight line and rotary labeling machines being key types. Straight line labeling machines operate in a linear fashion, applying labels to products as they move along a conveyor, ideal for simpler, lower-speed operations. In contrast, rotary labeling machines utilize a circular configuration, allowing for higher speeds and greater efficiency, suitable for larger production volumes. Both types offer flexibility, scalability, and customization to meet diverse labeling requirements across various industries.</p></p>
