<p><p>The off-the-road tire market encompasses three primary types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering flexibility and shock absorption, making them ideal for uneven terrains. Solid tires are made from solid rubber and provide durability and resistance to punctures, suitable for demanding industrial applications. Polyurethane tires combine the benefits of both, offering lightweight and resilient options with excellent wear resistance, making them ideal for indoor and outdoor use in varied environments.</p></p>
