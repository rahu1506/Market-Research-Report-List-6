<p><p>Aircraft Health Monitoring Systems (AHMS) are advanced technologies designed to enhance the safety and efficiency of aircraft operations by continuously assessing the condition and performance of various systems. Key market types include Diagnostics, which identifies existing problems; Prognostics, predicting future failures; Condition-based Monitoring, providing real-time assessments based on current operational conditions; and Adaptive Control, which adjusts system performance dynamically based on incoming health data. These systems collectively ensure optimal maintenance scheduling and improved aircraft reliability.</p></p>
