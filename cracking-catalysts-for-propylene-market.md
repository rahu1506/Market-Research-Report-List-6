<p><p>Cracking catalysts for the propylene market include several types: </p><p>1. **CHP (Continuous High-Performance)** catalysts enhance yield and selectivity for propylene in fluid catalytic cracking processes.</p><p>2. **CRP (Continuous Regenerative Process)** catalysts facilitate efficient regeneration, improving overall process stability.</p><p>3. **CIP (Catalytic Intermediate Production)** catalysts focus on producing feedstocks for downstream processes.</p><p>4. **MMC (Metallocene Catalysts)** are specialized for producing high-purity propylene derivatives.</p><p>5. **Others** encompass various niche catalysts tailored for specific refining needs, optimizing propylene production and efficiency across different applications.</p></p>
