<p><p>The automotive battery cables market is primarily segmented into three types: aluminium cables, copper cables, and alloy cables. Aluminium cables are lightweight and cost-effective but have higher resistance, making them less efficient for high-performance applications. Copper cables offer excellent conductivity and durability, making them the preferred choice for reliability and performance, albeit at a higher cost. Alloy cables combine the benefits of both metals, offering improved strength and conductivity while reducing weight, appealing to manufacturers seeking optimal performance and cost-efficiency.</p></p>
