<p><p>The scaffolding accessories market comprises various types catering to different construction needs. Single scaffolding features a single frame, ideal for bricklayers. Double scaffolding offers stability with parallel frameworks, suitable for masonry. Cantilever scaffolding extends outward for support on one side, perfect for limited ground space. Suspended scaffolding hangs from a structure, allowing access to high areas. Trestle scaffolding, supported by A-frames, is versatile for interior work. Steel scaffolding, known for durability, is widely used across all sectors for its strength and safety.</p></p>
