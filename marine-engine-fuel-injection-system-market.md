<p><p>The Marine Engine Fuel Injection System market consists of various systems enhancing fuel delivery and efficiency. The Pump-Line-Nozzle system relies on individual pumps for each cylinder, providing precise control of fuel injection. The Common Rail System, more advanced, utilizes a shared fuel rail to deliver fuel at high pressure to multiple injectors, improving performance and emissions. Other systems may include electronic fuel injection or hybrid methods, offering diverse options to meet regulatory standards and optimize engine performance in marine applications.</p></p>
