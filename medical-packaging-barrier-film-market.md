<p><ul><li>Metalized Barrier Films</li><li>Transparent Barrier Films</li><li>White Barrier Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical packaging barrier film market comprises three main types: metalized barrier films, transparent barrier films, and white barrier films. Metalized barrier films provide superior moisture and oxygen protection, ideal for products requiring long shelf lives. Transparent barrier films offer visibility for product identification while maintaining adequate protection, suitable for sterile applications. White barrier films, typically non-transparent, offer excellent light-blocking properties and are used when product visibility is not essential, providing an effective barrier against external contaminants.</p></p>
