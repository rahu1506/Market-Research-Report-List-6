<p><p>The water chiller market comprises several types, each suited for specific cooling applications. Screw chillers are known for their efficiency and reliability in industrial settings, utilizing twin-screw technology for effective refrigerant compression. Centrifugal chillers are ideal for large-scale operations, employing centrifugal force to achieve high cooling capacities with lower energy consumption. Reciprocating chillers utilize piston-driven mechanisms, making them suitable for smaller applications. Other market segments include absorption and thermoelectric chillers, which cater to niche requirements in various sectors.</p></p>
