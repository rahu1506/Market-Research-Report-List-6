<p><p>The Dual-Fuel Marine Engines market primarily encompasses two main engine types: Four-Stroke and Two-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines. Four-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines operate through a cycle of four distinct strokes, offering better flexibility and fuel efficiency, making them suitable for various maritime applications. Conversely, Two-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines utilize a simpler two-stroke cycle, providing higher power outputs and efficiency, ideal for larger vessels. Both types enable the use of alternative fuels, enhancing environmental sustainability in the marine sector.</p></p>
