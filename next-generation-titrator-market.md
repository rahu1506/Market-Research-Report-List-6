<p><strong>The Next-Generation Titrator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Titrator</li><li>Coulometric</li><li>Volumetric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Next-Generation Titrator market comprises three primary types: potentiometric, coulometric, and volumetric titrators. Potentiometric titrators measure the voltage change in a solution during a reaction, allowing for precise endpoint determination. Coulometric titrators analyze the amount of substance based on the electric charge passed through the solution, ideal for trace analysis. Volumetric titrators involve measuring the volume of titrant required to reach the endpoint, a traditional method suitable for various quantitative analyses in laboratories and industries.</p></p>
