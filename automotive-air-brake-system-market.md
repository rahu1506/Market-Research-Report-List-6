<p><ul><li>Air Disc</li><li>Air Drum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air brake system market is primarily segmented into two types: air disc and air drum brakes. Air disc brakes utilize a rotor and caliper design, providing superior stopping power and heat dissipation, making them well-suited for heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, air drum brakes consist of a drum and brake shoes, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, typically seen in lighter vehicles. Both systems harness compressed air for operation, ensuring efficient braking performance across various vehicle applications.</p></p>
