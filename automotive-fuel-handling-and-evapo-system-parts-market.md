<p><p>The automotive fuel handling and evaporative (EVAP) system market encompasses various components that manage fuel vapor emissions and maintain system efficiency. Key parts include the EVAP canister, which traps fuel vapors; the vent control valve, which regulates airflow in and out of the canister; and the purge valve/sensor that facilitates vapor flow into the engine for combustion. The liquid-vapor separator prevents liquid fuel from entering the EVAP system, while other parts include fittings and hoses that support overall functionality and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
