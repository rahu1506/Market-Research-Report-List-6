<p><p>The Ultrasonic Spray Coating System market features two main types: Rotary-Type and Flat-Type systems. Rotary-Type systems utilize a rotating atomizer to achieve uniform coating on cylindrical surfaces, making them ideal for applications such as cylindrical components. In contrast, Flat-Type systems employ a stationary nozzle to deliver coatings over flat surfaces, suitable for materials like films and panels. Both types leverage ultrasonic technology to produce fine, consistent spray patterns, enhancing coating quality and efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
